-----------KNOW MORE ABOUT US

We propose a full oriented system just for you !

Autism-Bridge is a website made by Algerian developers platform aiming for providing a full oriented system for autistic people, in the goal of integrating them the best way in the community by forming them and providing them with a lot of opportunities.

The idea is about bridging the gap between autistic individuals and society, while also emphasizing the focus on developing skills and creating opportunities for a brighter future. Our platform welcome you all, no matter your gender, age or preferences.
